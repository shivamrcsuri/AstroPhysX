Game Physics Information

Physics

Physics is the study of matter and motion through space and time. Rectillinear motion is motion along a straight line. The astroids, or obstacles presented in AstroPhysX's game move rectilinearly. Velocity is a vector quantity, which means that it has magnititude and direction. Speed is a scalar which means it is not dependent on direction. The rocket, represented by the rectangular prism can change velocity without changing its speed as it can change its direction to avoid contact with asteroids. Acceleration is another vector quantity which means to gain speed within a short amount of time. The astroids in this game, similar to outer space, travel at a constant speed due to the lack of gravity.
